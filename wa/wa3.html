Stray Kids

Stray Kids( abbreviation: SKZ) is a South Korean boy band formed by JYP Entertainment through the 2017 reality show - Stray Kids. The group's name "Stray Kids" was not defined by JYP Entertainment but conceptualized by the members themselves. It originally referred to a lost child who wants to chase their dreams and later evolved to represent the idea of finding a way together out of the ordinary.

Members

Stray Kids (스트레이 키즈) is a South Korean boy group under JYP Entertainment. The group consists of 8 members: Bang Chan, Lee Know, Changbin, Hyunjin, Han, Felix, Seungmin, and I.N. Stray Kids was created through the survival program with the same name, Stray Kids. Stray Kids debuted on March 25, 2018. As of February 10th, 2022, they are also signed under Republic Records.

Bang Chan Facts:

He was born in South Korea, but he moved to Sydney, Australia when he was very young. He has one younger sister (Hannah) and one younger brother (Lucas). Before leaving Sydney, he went to Newtown High School of the Performing Arts. He also went to Cheongdam High School. (SK-Talk Time 180422) His nicknames given by his members: Kangaroo and Koala. He joined JYP Entertainment in 2010 after passing an audition in Australia. He speaks English, Korean, Japanese and a bit of Chinese. Chan used to take ballet and modern dance classes. He thinks his charming point is his dimples when he smiles. His perfume is Versace Eros. His hobby is playing sports. He can play the guitar and the piano. His favorite season is autumn. (Summer Vacation VLive) He helps his members with producing songs. He was a trainee at JYP Entertainment for 7 years. He is friends with GOT7's BamBam and Yugyeom. He used to train with GOT7, TWICE, and DAY6. His stage name in 3RACHA is CB97. His role models are Drake, Cristiano Ronaldo, and his dad. Chan has a dog named Berry. If he wasn't in Stray Kids, he would be a kangaroo, actor or athlete. (VLive 180424) He starred in TWICE's “Like Ooh Ahh” MV as a zombie and Miss A's “Only You” MV. He revealed he is close friends with BLACKPINK's Lisa. They met during trainee days and have been friends since. His motto: “Just enjoy~” Chan's ideal type: he stated that he doesn't have an ideal type.

Lee Know Facts:

He was born in Gimpo, South Korea. He is an only child. He graduated from Gimpo Jeil Technical High School. He can speak basic English. He is ambidextrous. He has a unique and fun personality, a 4D personality. He was a trainee at JYP Entertainment for 1 year. He was a backup dancer for BTS. His hobbies are hiking, choreographing and watching movies. His favorite dance genre is hip-hop. His favorite season is autumn. (Summer Vacation VLive) His favorite color is mint. (Ceci Korea) His favorite song is 2PM's “10 out of 10“. His role model is 2PM's Taecyeon. His frequent habit is cracking his fingers. He has a scar on his tummy from a surgery he had as a kid. He described himself as a “cute person who eats well”. Minho has fear of heights. (The 9th Ep 2) Minho really likes to read and his favorite author is currently Keigo Higashino. Minho has 3 cats named Sooni, Doongi and Dori. If he wasn't in Stray Kids, he would be a dancer. (VLive 180424) He was eliminated on November 7, episode 4 of Mnet's Stray Kids but he was added back at the end of episode 9. His motto: “Let's eat well and live well.” He's an MC for “Show Music Core”. He casts in “Idol Dictation Contest”, the spin-off of tvN's variety show “Amazing Saturday”. He and Seungmin are fixed guests on KBS “BTOB Kiss the Radio”, every Monday. Lee Know's ideal type: A person who matches well with him and someone he can talk comfortably with.
